About Us

Welcome to my blog

Hello 👋 I’m Agrim Goyal, the creator behind this blogging platform. I’m currently pursuing my B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering, and this website started as small idea during my early college days. I always loved exploring new technologies, coding, and working on projects – but more than that, I loved sharing what I learned with others. That’s how TechTalks was born. 🌍 At first, it was just a simple place to post my notes, coding experiments, and thoughts on technology. Over time, it grew into something bigger – a space not just for me, but for anyone who wants to learn, explore, and grow in tech.

Our Journey & History

• 2025 (The Beginning): This website started as a side project – a way to document my coding journey and keep all my learning in one place.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Coding & Development Tutorials – Beginner to advanced concepts made simple.
  • Tech Trends & Insights – Breaking down the latest in technology.
  • Projects & Experiences – Real-world applications and personal learnings.
  • Guides & Resources – To help students and learners improve step by step.

Our Vision

This blog is not just about articles – it’s about building a community of learners. I believe knowledge grows when shared, and this platform is my way of giving back to the tech community while also improving myself every day. 👉 Whether you’re a beginner just starting your coding journey, a student working on projects, or someone curious about the world of technology, you’ll always find something here to learn and take away.

A Note From Me

This is still just the beginning. As the website grows, I plan to bring in more contributors, add new sections (like tutorials, projects, and tools), and make this platform a go-to place for learners. Thank you for being here and being a part of this journey. Feel free to explore, leave a comment, or connect with me – because learning is always better when we do it together.
